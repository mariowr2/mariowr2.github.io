		<p class="s5"><span class="s2">The Department of Computer Sciences is thrilled to announce that th</span><span class="s2">is year’s</span><span class="s2"> team from the Autonomous Agents Lab won first place in the Technical Challenges category at the 2018 </span><span class="s2">Robocup&nbsp;</span><span class="s2">Robotics Competitions</span><span class="s2"> h</span><span class="s2">eld in Montreal from June 16</span><span class="s3">th</span><span class="s2"> to 22</span><span class="s3">nd</span><span class="s2">. </span><span class="s2">The soccer portion of the competition includes four individual events, including push recovery, high jump, high kicks and goal kicks. </span><span class="s4">Unde</span><span class="s4">r the team leadership of post-doctoral fellow Dr. Meng Cheng Lau, AMN United overcame a number of hardware glitches including worn servo motors to come first in push recovery, goal kick, and high jump</span><span class="s4">, which is </span><span class="s4">their best result ever!</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
